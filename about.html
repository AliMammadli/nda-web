About Us

Every developed organization has own story. We help our clients establish their future.

Novum, founded in 2017, provides services in Data Analytics & Visualization, and Software Development. For many years, we have successfully delivered multiple projects, working within local and international markets. Due to increasing demand for process automation and complex data management, we offer customized solutions for each customer in order to meet market demands, e.g. reporting, agile decision-making, and workflow optimization in multiple industries.

Our primary aim is to bring decision-making and reporting to the next level, both visually and process-wisely by creating data-driven culture in corporate, business and functional layers of organization. As the Microsoft partner, we follow/implement all internationally recognized Microsoft Cloud and related technological structure to create smooth processes for our customers.

Because of multi-industrial experienced team members, those who have functioned at technology, aerospace, petroleum, non-profit, risk & finance and education, our team has passion to bring innovation & creativity. We help to our customers to achieve their goals through applying unique approaches.

Company vision

"Our vision is strongly based on transformation, planning and visualization"

We believe that everything starts with arrangement and transformation of data to more understandable beneficiary source. Delivering visual content instead of numbers and words, because the only thing people believe is what they see. Thus, will help companies to become more simple in interpretation of data and become part of inseparable technological progress.

Mission Statement

“To help the companies increase their ROI and efficiency through implementing data analytics' best practices.”

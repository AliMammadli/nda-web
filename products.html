Airports Turnaround Solution

Designed based on Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning technology to increase the efficiency of turnaround process at airports. From the moment an aircraft lands to the minute it leaves, with Novum’s Turnaround Management, you’ll have real-time visibility across all turnaround activities with real-time information easily accessible from central control center. All real-time turnaround information can be viewed in a simple yet powerful application. Easy to use and navigate, this application gives a clear overview of all operations, all in one place. It can be a great tool to monitor and evaluate cost and efficiency of ground handling vendor, automatic invoices can be generated based on hourly rates etc.

NES (NOVUM ENTRANCE SYSTEM)

Novum Entrance System is unique web-based business application developed on .NET Core and compatible with Microsoft Azure predesigned for business centers with large volume of guests, providing professional services for companies. System is especially indispensable for large companies and networks, as it greatly simplifies and speeds up guest registration with multiple functions such as defining access range (date & time), with supervising details. Companies can pre-register their guests and entrance process become seamless, saving time and adds flexibility.

OCR – Certification Directory

Stands for "Optical Character Recognition." It is a technology that recognizes text within a digital image. Procedure that many companies and enterprises need, because large archives in paper form will sooner or later have to be converted to digital format. This is a unique complex software that uses an optical text recognition system of any level of complexity - from questionnaires or brochures to manuscripts and images. Applied in industries such as Government and Non-Government organizations in order to store/track regulatory certificates, invoices, contracts, reports and documentation in digital format

Applications in business: Government Documentation etc. Regulatory Certificates.

Face Recognition & Face Detection

In recent years, computer vision has gained special attention. Face recognition technology can be used to count/identify the number of people attending an event, building or shops. This will help automate the process and save time. In a face detection module, you get a database of detected faces for the required time interval and for the necessary cameras. The face recognition module is designed to automatically identify faces from a video image. Optimal face images (reference images) are automatically extracted from the video stream and stored in a database for subsequent identification. High recognition accuracy is ensured using innovative indexing technology based on facial biometric parameters.

Commodity Trading Platform

The commodity trading platform is a web app (SaaS), that has a wide range of customizability as Admin can add different types of products with specifications, create custom approval flows. Salespersons can identify the timeline of the trade with or without min amount applied, add related documents and comments for the product and use the build-in analytical tool for monitoring and decision making.

Convenient and Structured approach to the whole process
